Predicting the Behavior of Financial Markets from News Headlines

Behavioral Economics believe in importance of emotions in human decision making process and subsequently its influence on market through investors and social mood.Three measures were selected as market indicators including Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA), the S&P 500 Volatility Index (VIX) and gold price. The data for these indicators was collected from Yahoo Finance website and http://www.gold.org/ website for the years of 2008 to 2016.
